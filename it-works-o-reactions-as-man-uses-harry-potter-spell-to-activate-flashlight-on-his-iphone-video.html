It Works O- Reactions as Man Uses Harry Potter Spell to Activate Flashlight on His iPhone [VID

 Posted on May 16, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  150 words  |  Aldo Pusey


Popular Harry Potter’s spell from the fictional movie has come alive after a Nigerian man used one of the spell in the cult movie to functionalize the flashlight of his smartphone.

He made the attempt after a report by Uber Facts that the spell ”Lumos” when spoken with voice command, can turn on iPhone and Android flashlights.

Lumos is a magical spell from Harry Potter films and books, which enables the tip of the wand of the person who is casting the spell to light up when said and serve as a flashlight, enabling the magician to see in the dark.

To confirm if the aforementioned is actually true, this young man put it to the test and it worked perfectly with his iPhone.

 He shared information of his discovery on social media writing; ”Hey Siri” and a second later he said ”Lumos” after which the flashlight became active.
