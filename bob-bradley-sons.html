Bob Bradley Sons: Meet Michael And Kerry: Bob Bradley, officially known as Robert Frank Bradley was born on March 3, 1958, in Montclair, New Jersey.

He’s an American soccer coach who most recently was the head coach and sporting director of Toronto FC, a Canadian professional soccer club.

Bradley coached in the American college game and Major League Soccer (MLS), managing the Chicago Fire, MetroStars, and Chivas USA over nine seasons.

In 2006, he was appointed manager of the United States men’s team winning the CONCACAF Gold Cup in 2007 and finishing runner-up in 2009 and 2011.

His team (the United States men’s team) also won the 2009 FIFA Confederations Cup and also reached the last 16 of the 2010 FIFA World Cup. He then managed Egypt.

Bradley became the first American to manage a team in a European first division with Stabæk of Norway in 2014.

Later he moved on to French club Le Havre and Welsh side Swansea City, becoming the first American to manage a Premier League club.

Bradley returned to the MLS to manage Los Angeles FC and Toronto FC after he was fired less than three months after managing Swansea City

As a soccer coach, he has had a stint with a number of soccer teams and clubs including Egypt, Stabæk Fotball, Le Havre AC, Swansea City, Los Angeles FC, and Toronto FC.

In September 2011, Bradley reached a deal to manage the Egypt national team beginning October 15, 2011. He made his debut on November 14, in a friendly against Brazil.

On January 2, 2014, Bradley agreed to manage Stabæk Football in Norway, making him the first American to manage a club in a European top flight

On November 10, 2015, Bradley was officially named as the new manager of French Ligue 2 side Le Havre AC, signing a two-year contract

On October 3, 2016, following the dismissal of Francesco Guidolin, Bradley was appointed as the manager of Swansea City.

On July 27, 2017, Bradley was announced as the team’s first head coach for the inaugural season of Los Angeles FC, an MLS team that started playing in the 2018 season.

On November 18, 2021, following Los Angeles FC’s failure to qualify for the playoffs for the first time, Bradley and Los Angeles FC parted ways.

A few days later (November 24, 2021), he was announced as the head coach and sporting director of Toronto FC where he reunited with his son Michael, who has been the club’s captain since 2015.

In June 2023, Bradley hit major headlines after Toronto FC announced it had parted ways with Bradley after a 3W-10D-4L record to start the season

This comes after Toronto FC lost 2-1 to New England Revolution on Saturday, June 24. The club also fired Mike Sorber, Bradley’s right-hand man who was both assistant coach and technical director.

Bob Bradley Sons: Meet Michael And Kerry

Bob Bradley is blessed with three children; a daughter named Ryan Bradley and two sons; Michael Bradley, and Kerry Bradley.

The American soccer coach shares all three children with his wife, Lindsay Bradley, a former University of Virginia lacrosse player.

Michael was born on July 31, 1987. He’s an American professional soccer player who began his professional career with the MetroStars in Major League Soccer in 2004

At the time of this writing, he plays as a midfielder and captains Toronto FC in Major League Soccer. He formerly captained the United States national team.

Bob’s daughter, Ryan is married to Andy Rose, a former professional soccer player for Vancouver Whitecaps FC and current assistant coach for Seattle Sounders.
