Former Finnish Prime Minister Sanna Marin Embraces New Style and Lifestyle Following Divorce and Gov

 Posted on September 10, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  209 words  |  Aldo Pusey

After stepping down from her position as the Prime Minister of Finland and going through a divorce, the vibrant former leader Sanna Marin traded her formal pantsuits for trendy party dresses and crop tops during the Flow Festival in Helsinki.

At 37, Marin, who holds the distinction of being the youngest female world leader in history, showcased her fashionable side in various stylish outfits as she indulged in a politics-free lifestyle and her “first proper summer vacation” in a while.

Following her divorce in May, Marin embraced a bold look by donning a sheer black dress.

On the following day, Marin added a pop of color to her attire, appearing in a hot pink mini dress paired with thigh-high black boots.

The former PM also shared a photo of herself sporting a pink tye-dye crop top, pleather miniskirt, and chunky black heels.

Marin wrote on Instagram, “This summer I’ve had a proper summer vacation for the first time in a while. It has included, among other things, being busy with [5-year-old daughter] Emma, sports, friends, good food, and unforgettable trips.”

She faced criticism for her partying and her government’s big spending.

Throughout her four-year tenure, there were rumors that her lively social life was affecting her job performance.
