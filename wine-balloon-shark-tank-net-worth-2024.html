The Wine Balloon: A Shark Tank Success Story

The Wine Balloon, presented to the Sharks by entrepreneur Eric Corti, is a device that aims to keep wine fresh after the bottle has been opened. The product, which uses a balloon that inflates inside the bottle to minimize air exposure, caught the attention of the Sharks and has since become a popular accessory among wine lovers.

Appearance on Shark Tank

Eric Corti’s pitch on Shark Tank was a memorable one. He demonstrated how the Wine Balloon could effectively seal a bottle of wine, preserving its taste for days.
