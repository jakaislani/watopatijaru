Adnan Shah aka Adnan Shah Tipu is a Pakistani actor who is popular for his portrayal in the most-watched television drama serial titled ‘Suno Chanda’ starring Farhan Khan and Iqra Aziz as lead stars. Also, he is famous for performing comic and villainous roles as well.

Adnan Shah is not just acted in TV serials, yet in movies as well. Moreover, he has made his Bollywood debut with ‘Kajrare’ which was released in the year 2010, directed by Pooja Bhatt. He is such a talented and versatile actor.

Career

In 1999, Adnan Shah started his acting career as a TV actor and earned fame after appearing in a sitcom named ‘Sab Set Hai’ which was aired on Indus Vision in 2001. Since then, he made various appearances on many TV serials in Pakistan, such as; ‘Makan’, (2006), ‘Kis Din Mera Viyah Howay Ga’, and many others.

In 2003, Tipu Shah made his debut with a film named ‘Khamosh Pani’. In 2008, he also made an appearance in a drama film named ‘Ramchand Pakistani’.

Among many other acting credits,’Dukhtar’ (2014), ‘Suno Chanda’, ‘and many others are popular because of his well-praised performances.

Achievements

Tipu rose to fame for giving the best performance in ‘Josh: Independence Through Unity’ and it led him to earn a nomination in ARY Film Award for Best Actor.
