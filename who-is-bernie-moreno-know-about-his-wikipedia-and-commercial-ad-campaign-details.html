Bernie Moreno is included on the Wikipedia page of the 2022 United States Senate election in Ohio. What is his net worth?

Moreno is an American businessman and politician from Ohio. He is a candidate of the U.S. Senate representing Ohio state. He will compete with other GOP Senate candidates in the general election on May 3, 2022.

He runs over $1 billion businesses in the state. He was awarded the CSU Presidential Medal in 2021 by Cleveland State University.

Is Bernie Moreno On Wikipedia?

Although a well-known businessman, Bernie Moreno is not yet on Wikipedia. He is enlisted as the declared candidate for the Republican primary on the Wikipedia page of the 2022 United States Senate election in Ohio.

However, Bernie has a LinkedIn profile which states his education and career details. He completed his schooling at Pine Crest High School in 1985. He was an active student who was a part of the tennis team and the swim team.

He graduated from the University of Michigan in 1989 with a degree in BBA Marketing. While in college, he founded Automotive Industry Club.

Currently, he is the president of Bernie Moreno Companies and Infiniti of Coral Gables. He is the Chairman of the technology software incubator company, Ownum.

Moreno is affiliated with Cleveland State University, Rock and Roll Hall of Fame, InnovateOhio, and Blue Coats.

Bernie announced his candidacy for the United States Senate in April 2021.

Bernie Moreno Net Worth Details Revealed

Bernie Moreno’s net worth might be more than $100 million. Moreno owns condos in Colombus, New York City, and Washington D.C. He also underpins cryptocurrency and has invested over $100k in Bitcoin.

He has household assets worth $20.3 million to $93.1 million. He owns a house in the Bahamas and several commercial real estate developments in the Cleveland area, reports Cleveland.com.

Bernie made millions from capital gains, royalties, and rent from his commercial properties.

Meet Bernie Moreno Wife Bridget Moreno

Bernie Moreno and his wife Bridget Moreno have been married for over thirty years. The couple has four children. He currently lives in Westlake, Ohio, USA, with his wife and family.

Talking about Moreno’s religion, he is a Catholic. He was born in Bogota, Colombia, and came to the United States when he was young.

Bernie Moreno Commercial Ad Campaign

Bernie Moreno launched an unprecedented Ohio TV Ad Campaign on December 1, 2021. The commercial is called “Gas.”

Moreno announced that his campaign will spend around $4 million on TV ads till January 15. The campaign asserted that it is the largest most concentrated ad campaign in Ohio, reports Cleveland Scene.
